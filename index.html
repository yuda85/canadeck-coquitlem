our service

Deck Design and Build:

Our experts will team will design a custom deck that fits your specific needs and preferences. We use state-of-the-art design software to create detailed plans and drawings, so you can see exactly what your new deck will look like before we start building. We work with a variety of materials, including composite, PVC, cedar and pressure-treated wood to ensure that your new deck is beautiful, durable, and long-lasting.

Deck Repairs and Maintenance:

We provide deck repairs and maintence service. If your existing deck is in need of repairs or maintenance, our team is here to help. We can fix any structural issues, replace damaged boards, and perform any necessary maintenance to ensure that your deck is safe and secure. We also offer regular deck maintenance services, such as staining and sealing, to protect your deck from the elements and keep it looking great for years to come.

Deck Railing and Staircase Installation:

We offer a wide variety of railing and staircase options to help you create the perfect outdoor space for your home. From traditional wood railings to modern glass, aluminum or cable railings, we have a style to suit any taste. Our team of experts will work with you to design and install a railing or staircase that is both functional and beautiful.

About CanaDeck

Over 15 Years Experience in This Industry


Canadeck is a name synonymous with quality, craftsmanship, and innovation in the custom deck industry. With 15 years of experience in designing and building custom decks, we are proud to be one of the most reputable and reliable deck companies in the market.

We specialize in designing and building custom decks that enhance the beauty and functionality of your outdoor space. Our skilled and experienced team has a passion for creating unique and beautiful decking solutions that are tailored to your specific needs and preferences.
